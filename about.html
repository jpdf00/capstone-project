International Conference on
Renewable Energy 2020

Renewable Energy forms one of the pillars for delivering a global sustainable environment and the commitment to climate change targets. The renewable energy industry has matured, with huge investments being ploughed into it globally. ICREN 2020 will include articles and presentations on the latest research on renewable energy technologies, grid interactions, energy efficiency, data analytics, economics and finance, environmental and social impact as well as policy and climate change implications.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about ICREN 2020

icren2020@premc.org

2020 Logo

The logo for ICREN 2020 was choosen to represent the need to represent the need for sustainability in how we produce our energy.

After consultation from several designs and professionals in the field the lightbulb with a leaf was choosen.

The logo can be presented in a variety of colors for better integration with different designs.
